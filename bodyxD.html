Why Cloudi?

- A powerful computer at your fingertips -
Cloudi is a scalable, multi-platform service that allows you to create and access a personal computer system from any device, and at any time. Before Cloudi, running graphics/CPU-heavy apps on a portable device (laptop, phone, tablet) was nearly impossible. Now, you can do all of that regardless of your devices' processing power, hardware, etc.
